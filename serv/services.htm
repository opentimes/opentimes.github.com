  • Broadcasting commercials on the giant LED screen downtown Alba Iulia, with full time internet monitoring
 
 
 The location of our LED technology video display is in the city center of Alba Iulia, in the financial and administrative hearth of our city, in the intersection of the main roads (see Google Map). Its size is 4x3 m, virtual pitch of 10 mm, real pitch of 20 mm and best viewing range between 10 - 150 m. It is computer controlled, it incorporates a professional "Prosound 400" sound system, it is able to display 16.7 million colors and any TV commercials, live news, complex messages with high creativity potential. It is a real magnet for the passers-by sights: it is outstanding by aspect, animation, colors and sound. The viewers have time enough to observe the LED screen and get its message. It is a catchily presence, offering an excellent visibility to all the traffic participants: pedestrians, as well as vehicle passengers.
